Meet Mischief [いたずら]

The newest and most colourful collection from Tsuki is here.

“When we start designing a new collection, we always like to envision who would be wearing it. For Mischief it was someone bold and cheeky - not afraid to sport the colour and fun that went in these pieces! Before we gave this new collection a name, we knew a little mascot was a must. Multiple drawings later, we both loved the mischievous look of the cat character best. With that, Tsuki Mischief was made!”

- Felix & Marzia Kjellberg

Co-Founders & Creative Directors
